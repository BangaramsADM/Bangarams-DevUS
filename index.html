Meet Our Makers

Two trusted specialists — a master goldsmith and a certified diamond supplier — cover every tier and every customization.

RAMOUJI
Master Goldsmith · Silver & Gold
Silver, Gold & Custom Jewelry Craftsman

Ramouji is our trusted goldsmith — working in both silver and gold with equal mastery. Whether you want an ornate 22K heirloom bridal set or a gold-plated silver piece that photographs like the real thing, Ramouji brings the same dedication to every design. He is the reason Bangarams can offer genuine customization at every price point.

KIRA
Certified Diamond Supplier · B2B
Lab-Grown & Natural Diamond Specialist

Kira is our exclusive B2B diamond partner — offering both lab-grown and natural diamonds with full GIA and IGI documentation. Lab-grown diamonds are chemically identical to natural stones at 40–60% lower cost. Kira also offers personalized stone selection consultations so you choose the exact cut, clarity, and carat that fits your vision.
